# TuringPost Social Media Management

## About TuringPost

TuringPost is a newsletter on AI & ML covering weekly trends, LLM/FM insights, AI unicorn profiles, global affairs, and historical analysis.

## My Role

I managed TuringPost's social media presence across Twitter and LinkedIn, developing content strategy and community engagement to support newsletter growth and brand awareness.

## Key Responsibilities

### Social Media Strategy

- **Platform management**: managed Twitter and LinkedIn accounts with focus on audience engagement and subscriber acquisition
- **Content strategy**: developed and executed full-cycle content strategy to enhance audience engagement and brand awareness
- **Performance optimization**: continually optimized content approach based on performance analytics and engagement metrics
- **Community engagement**: actively engaged with the online AI/ML community to increase interactions and drive profile visits

### Team Development

- **Intern mentoring**: streamlined onboarding and mentoring of a new social media intern
- **Process development**: created structured processes that enabled intern to achieve near-full autonomy within one month
- **Strategy leadership**: led weekly strategy reviews to refine social media approaches and improve performance

## Results

### Growth Metrics

- **Twitter**: 46K followers ➡️ 60K followers (30% increase)
- **LinkedIn**: 2.6K followers ➡️ 4.6K followers (76% increase)
- **Subscriber acquisition**: established social media as the 3rd largest source of new newsletter subscribers

### Operational Improvements

- **Team scalability**: successfully onboarded and developed intern to handle significant portion of social media operations
- **Process efficiency**: created repeatable systems for content creation and community engagement
- **Strategic alignment**: aligned social media activities with overall newsletter growth objectives

## Strategy & Approach

### Content Development

- **Audience-focused content**: created content tailored to AI/ML professionals and researchers
- **Brand consistency**: maintained TuringPost's voice and expertise positioning across platforms
- **Engagement optimization**: developed content formats that encouraged community interaction and sharing

### Analytics & Optimization

- **Performance tracking**: monitored engagement metrics, follower growth, and subscriber conversion rates
- **Data-driven decisions**: used analytics to inform content strategy and posting optimization
- **Conversion focus**: optimized social media funnel to drive newsletter subscriptions

### Community Building

- **Industry engagement**: participated in AI/ML discussions and conversations within the professional community
- **Relationship building**: fostered connections with researchers, practitioners, and industry professionals
- **Thought leadership**: positioned TuringPost as a valuable voice in AI/ML discourse

## Impact

Successfully transformed TuringPost's social media presence into a significant subscriber acquisition channel while building an engaged community around AI/ML content. Developed scalable processes and team capabilities that supported sustained growth and community engagement.
