# Case Study: Growing TheSequence's Social Media Presence

## About TheSequence

TheSequence is an ML community media, trusted by over 150,000+ specialists from all over the world, including the top AI labs, and hundreds of large enterprises.

## My Role

I managed the day-to-day execution of TheSequence's social media presence (Twitter and LinkedIn), handling content creation and scheduling while collaborating with my manager on strategy development and weekly performance reviews.

## Key Responsibilities

### Daily Execution

- **Account management**: researched and created content for Twitter and LinkedIn platforms
- **Content development**: developed engaging posts tailored to each platform's audience
- **Trend monitoring**: monitored industry trends and AI/ML updates to ensure timely and relevant content
- **Analytics collection**: gathered weekly KPIs and performance data for strategy sessions

### Collaborative Strategy and Analysis

- **Weekly performance reviews**: collected analytics and tracked KPIs weekly with manager to analyze and find the best-performing posts and formats
- **Data-driven optimization**: introduced weekly changes in the content plan according to analytics insights
- **Content experimentation**: brainstormed ideas about formats and content, researched fast-growing accounts to learn from them, and experimented with new approaches
- **Partnership negotiations**: collaborated on negotiations with communities and companies operating within the industry

## Results

- **Twitter**: 500 followers ➡️ 46,000 followers (92x growth)
- **LinkedIn**: 0 followers ➡️ 2,600 followers (built from scratch)
- **Engagement**: consistently increased engagement through data-driven content adjustments

## Strategy

- Weekly KPI tracking and analysis
- A/B testing of content formats
- Performance-based content optimization
- Competitive analysis and benchmarking

## Impact

Contributed to establishing TheSequence as a go-to resource for ML professionals, helping attract attention from leading AI labs and major enterprises while supporting the growth of an engaged community of researchers and practitioners.
